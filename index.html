A small lab. Between sea, code, and verse.

Rocket Science Lab is where Łukasz "kapoost" Kapuśniak builds things at the edge of ad infrastructure, agentic protocols, and the indie web — when he is not at sea or writing the next four lines that won't sit still.

What lives here

This site is the public face of a working studio. The projects below are real, in production or in active development. Each one started as a question that wouldn't go away.

  • Purrsonality — a cat-personality quiz that doubles as a production AdCP seller agent. Quiet experiment, real publishers, real ad calls.
  • AdCP work — agents, signal providers, seller-side conformance work on the Ad Context Protocol.
  • humanMCP — a personal MCP server, because identity belongs to the person, not the platform.
  • Writing — short poems, mostly Polish, mostly about distance.

Why "rocket science"

Because most things called rocket science aren't, and most things that quietly are — naming, error handling, getting the second column of a small CSV right — never get called that. This is a lab for taking the small things seriously.
